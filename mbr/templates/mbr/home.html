MeteoBriefing New

MeteoBriefing is automated pre-flight information system intended to supply and display meteorological information to flight crew members and operators for self-briefing, flights planning and flight documentation purposes.

You should contact the service provider SE "UAMC" for authorization in the system by obtaining the registration information containing user name and password. Contact information is stated in the bottom of every page of the site.

Meteorological information is provided on the contractual basis.
